WOMEN IN HISTORY

You may have searched the web, books or any other
medium for information about the great minds that
paved the road to the fate of the world we live in tod-
ay. Most of these lists consists mostly or even comp-
letely of males. Does that mean women didn't contri-

bute to the innovations, art, leadership and discover-
ies that got us to where we are today? No. Without th-
em humankind wouldn't have achieved half the things they have.
This website was made to honor these women and give
them the recognition they deserve.
